Popup Pops (summer business startup)

I partnered up with friends to build and launch an organic popsicle company. We built the brand from zero, firstly developing each unique flavor. I also designed appealing social media posts using Photoshop. We recruited a team of enthusiastic individuals to help with sales and distribution on busy beaches, farmers markets, and parks. We streamlined the entire process and enhanced the product based on customer and team feedback. Ultimately, in a short 3 months, we achieved $10,000 in sales.
